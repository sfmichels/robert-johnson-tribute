Robert Johnson

...the first modern blues man"

Rock and Roll Hall of Fame

The King of the Delta Blues, Robert Johnson wrote 29 songs in his short life. Many of them have become classics in blues, country and in rock and roll. The best way to show tribute to Robert Johnson is to show how his songs are still being played. Here is a sample of some of the musicians that have covered Robert Johnson songs.

  • Kind Hearted Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Rory Bloc, Robert Lockwood Jr., David Bromberg, George Thorogood, Keb'Mo.
  • Sweet Home Chicago
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, The Blues Brothers, Magic Sam, Johnny Otis, Fleetwood Mac, Honey Boy Edwards, (Barack Obama, Feb 21, 2012 in the East Room of the White House with Buddy Guy, B.B. King, Mick Jagger and Guy Clark Jr.
  • I believe I'll Dust My Broom
    • Covers: The Yardbirds, Ike and Tina Turner, Elmore James, Howlin' Wolf, Canned Heat, Todd Rungren, Casandra Wilson
  • Ramblin' On My Mind
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Lucinda Williams, Marshall Tucker Band, Savoy Brown, Arthur "Big Boy" Crudup, John Mayall and the Blues Breakers
  • When You Got a Good Friend
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Johnny Winter, Rolling Stones, Lady Antebellum
  • Come On In My Kitchen
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Delaney and Bonnie with Duane Allman on Slide Guitar, Casandra Wilson, Pattie Smith, Rory Block, David Bromberg. George Harrison, Leon Russel, Eric Clapton, and Ringo Starr performed it at The Concert For Bangladesh.
  • Terraplane Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, John Lee Hooker, Roy Rogers, Rory Block, Canned Heat, Robert Lockwood Jr., Fog Hat
  • Phonograph Blues
    • Paul James, The White Stripes, John Hammond, Vassar Clements (featuring Roy Rogers), Alexis Korner
  • 32-20 Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Bob Dylan, Alexis Korner, Muddy Waters, The New York Dolls, Flamin' Groovies, Gov't Mule, Keith Richards, The Cowboy Junkies
  • They're Red Hot
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Casandra Wilson (as Hot Tamales), Red Hot Chili Peppers, Hugh Laurie, Peter Green Splinter Group
  • Dead Shrimp Blues
    • Covers: Blind Willie Dukes, Paul Black & The Flip Kings
  • Crossroads Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Cream, Elmore James, Joe Bonamassa, Ry Cooder, Phish, Stevem Stills
  • Walkin' Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, R.L. Burnside, Johnny Cash, Grateful Dead, Hot Tuna, Skip James, Bonnie Raitt, Quicksliver Messenger Service, Todd Rungren, Roy Rogers
  • Last Fair Deal Gone Done
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Keb Mo, Beck, Taj Mahal and Ry Cooder (Rising Sons), Rory Block
  • Preachin' Blues(Up Jumped the Devil)
    • Covers: Derek Trucks, Allman Brothers, John Hammond, Gun Club
  • If I Had Possession Over Judgment Day
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Blues Etilicos, Gov't Mule, Pat Travers, Sugar Ray and the Blue Tones, Asylum Street Spankers
  • Stones in My Passway
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Melanie Mason, Zakiya Hooker, John Mellencamp, Joe Bonamassa, White Stripes
  • I'm a Steady Rollin' Man
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Peter Green Splinter Group, George Thorogood, Peter Kaukonen & John Sheehan
  • From Four Till Late
    • Covers: Cream, Peter Green Splinter Group, Hot Tuna, Elvis Costello
  • Hellhound on My Trail
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Cassandra Wilson, Fleetwood Mac, Rory Block, Big Joe Williams, Alexis Korner, Leslie West, Alvin Youngblood Hart with James Cotton, Todd Rundgren
  • Little Queen of Spades
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Derek Trucks (Solo), Robert Junior Lockwood, Sarah Dash/Cory Glover
  • Malted Milk
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Robert Lockwood Junior, Sadie Johnson & Joey Crumbs, Lucinda Williams
  • Drunken Hearted Man
    • Covers: Peter Green Splinter Group
  • Me and the Devil Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Mikey Junior, Cowboy Junkies, Widespread Panic, Mick Jagger(solo), Gil Scott-Heron, The Doors
  • Stop Breakin' Down Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Robert Lockwood Jr., ZZ Top, Widespread Panic, White Stripes, Lucinda Williams, Rolling Stones, Peter Green, Sonny Boy Williamson, Waylon Jenning, Jeff Healey Band
  • Traveling Riverside Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Led Zeppelin, Todd Rungren, Peter Green, John Hammond, Warren Zevon / REM, Robben Ford
  • Honeymoon Blues
    • Covers: John Hammond, Bessie Smith, Peter Green Splinter Group
  • Love in Vain
    • Covers: Rolling Stones, Eric Clapton, Keb' Mo, The Faces(with Ronnie Wood), Sonny Boy Williamson, Stevie Ray Vaughn, Madeleine Peyroux, Mick Taylor, Dogbowl Stephen Tooney
  • Milkcow's Calf Blues
    • Covers: Eric Clapton, Pyeng Threadgill, Rory Block, John Edwards, John Hammond, Bob Dylan
